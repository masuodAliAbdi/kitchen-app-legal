Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

This policy explains what information Kitchen App ("the App") collects, how it's used, and how it's protected. The App is a kitchen management tool used by restaurant and kitchen staff to manage recipes, inventory, suppliers, stock records, and tasks.

Information we collect

  • Account information: username, email address, and full name (optional), used to create and secure your account.
  • Kitchen operational data: recipes, ingredients, inventory items, stock batches, suppliers, sales and waste records, and tasks that you or your kitchen's staff enter into the App.
  • Usage data: basic account activity (such as sign-in times) needed to keep your account secure and to enforce access permissions.

How we use this information

  • To provide and operate the App's features for your kitchen.
  • To authenticate you and enforce the permissions your kitchen owner has granted you.
  • To send essential account emails (password reset, account invites) — never marketing email.

We do not sell your data to third parties, and we do not use advertising or third-party tracking SDKs in the App.

Where your data is stored

Data is stored using Supabase, a hosted database and authentication provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Supabase's infrastructure.

Data retention

When you delete a recipe, inventory item, supplier, or other record, it is held in a recoverable state for 30 days before being permanently erased. Deactivated or removed user accounts are handled the same way.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at the email below. Kitchen owners can also manage and remove staff access directly within the App.

Children's privacy

The App is intended for use by kitchen staff and business owners and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised effective date.
